Our Modules: The digital software toolkit for your SME

Flexible solutions that perfectly match your company size and needs

Core Modules

The three building blocks of KMUpower Suite form the foundation for your digital transformation.

CRM (Customer Management)

Centrally manage customer, prospect, and supplier data. Includes contact categorization. Forms the basis for invoicing, mailing campaigns, and support.

+ Optional: Quote Management + Optional: Conversation/Email History

Mailing/Newsletter

Send newsletters or promotional emails to defined recipient groups. With CRM integration for effective use of customer segments for systematic marketing and increased customer loyalty.

+ Optional: Sequence Emails (automated email series)

Employee Management

Central management of employee master data (name, department, role, contact details). Forms the basis for HR-related processes with less administrative effort and faster organization of personnel information.

Extension Modules (Add-ons)

Extend your KMUpower Suite as needed with additional modules.

Time Tracking

Record working hours (check-in/check-out) and breaks precisely.

Prerequisite: Employee Management Note: Project Management module is additionally required for project hours

Project Management

Plan and coordinate projects with task and resource planning. With Teams integration for efficient collaboration.

Prerequisite: Employee Management Optional: Connection with CRM (for customer-specific projects)

Vacation/
Absence Management

Digital vacation requests and absence management with approval workflow by supervisors.

Prerequisite: Employee Management

Expense Management

Record receipts and implement an approval workflow for efficient expense management.

Prerequisite: Employee Management

Purchase & Inventory

Centrally manage orders, supplier information, and inventory levels. Keep track of goods receipt and receive inventory warnings.

Optional: Using CRM for suppliers as 'contacts'

Onboarding / Offboarding

Automated tasks and information provision for new and departing employees.

Prerequisite: Employee Management

Invoicing / Billing

Create professional invoices from quotes with automated PDF delivery and payment tracking.

Prerequisite: Quote Management

Service Portal / Support

Ticket system for customer or internal requests with structured tracking and processing.

Prerequisite: CRM (external tickets) or Employee Management (internal)

Teams Manager

Central requesting and creation of Microsoft Teams & access permissions. With dynamic folder structures and automatic document creation.

Technical Foundation

Microsoft 365

The KMUpower Suite is based on Microsoft 365 and uses:

  • SharePoint for data and documents
  • Teams for channels, chats, and project work
  • Outlook for email and calendar

Each user needs a Microsoft 365 license (Business Standard or higher)

Power Platform

Extended functionality through:

  • Power Apps for interactive applications
  • Power Automate for workflows, notifications, and approvals

Continuous updates ensure steady improvements without major system changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions about our solutions

What does modular business solution mean? +

Our modular business solution allows you to select exactly the functions you actually need. You only pay for what you really use, and you can add more modules at any time as your business grows or your requirements change.

How long does implementation take? +

Implementation time depends on the number of modules. We calculate about one hour for basic implementation per module. For our 3-module bundle, the total implementation is about 2-3 hours (including basic setup). The system is ready for use immediately after implementation.

What are the benefits of the maintenance contract? +

With our support & maintenance contracts (retainers), you not only secure guaranteed response times and support hours, but also benefit from discounted prices for additional modules (300 €/CHF instead of up to 500 €/CHF) with the Basic and Pro packages. In the Premium package, additional modules are even free. We ensure that your applications always run smoothly.

Can I add modules later? +

Yes, you can add more modules to your existing KMUpower Suite at any time. The prices are tiered: The 1st module costs 1,000 €/CHF, the 2nd 900 €/CHF, ..., from the 6th module onwards 500 €/CHF each. If you have a Basic or Pro maintenance contract, you pay only 300 €/CHF for each additional module. With the Premium retainer, additional modules are free.

How long do the maintenance contracts run? +

Our support and maintenance contracts (retainers) have a minimum term of 6 months. After this period, they can usually be terminated with a notice period (e.g. monthly or quarterly, details in the contract). We recommend a longer-term partnership to continuously benefit from support, updates, and advantages.

What technology is the KMUpower Suite based on? +

The KMUpower Suite is based on Microsoft 365 and the Power Platform. It uses SharePoint for data and documents, Teams for collaboration, and the Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate) for applications and workflows. Each user needs a Microsoft 365 license (Business Standard or higher) to fully use the suite.

What customizations are possible outside the modules? +

Changes that go beyond the standard scope of a module are charged additionally. For this, you can use our retainer models or hour packages. The individual hourly rate is 225 €/CHF, we also offer packages with 10 hours (2,100 €/CHF) or 20 hours (4,000 €/CHF).

How do the dependencies between modules work? +

Some modules build on each other to ensure all data is properly linked. For example, Time Tracking requires the Employee Management module. Project hours can only be recorded if both Time Tracking and Project Management are available. Invoicing requires Quote Management. We'll be happy to advise you on the optimal combinations for your requirements when selecting modules.

What are the technical requirements? +

The KMUpower Suite is based on Microsoft 365 and the Power Platform. Each user needs a Microsoft 365 license (Business Standard or higher) to fully use the suite. The solution uses SharePoint for data and documents, Teams for collaboration, and the Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate) for applications and workflows. No additional server installations or complex IT infrastructure is required.

How does the implementation of KMUpower Suite work? +

Implementation takes place in three steps: 1) Analysis conversation to determine exact needs and clarify modules. 2) Implementation of the core package or desired add-ons. 3) Individual customizations via retainer or hour packages if needed. The core package can usually be deployed within 4 weeks, allowing you to quickly benefit from the solution.
